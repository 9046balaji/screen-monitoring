# DigiWell Demo Script (3 Min)

## Roles:
- **Presenter:** Drives the UI and talks through the problem/solution.

## 0:00 - 0:30 (Problem & Overview)
- Start on the **Dashboard**.
- **Script:** "Like millions of others, I spend 6 hours a day doomscrolling. We built DigiWell, not just to track time, but to proactively intervene using AI. Where others stop at descriptive analytics, we focus on predictive intervention and cognitive behavioral therapy."

## 0:30 - 1:15 (Wellness Tips & Relapse Predictor)
- Navigate to **Wellness**.
- Show the **Relapse & Doomscroll Predictor**. 
- **Script:** "Our AI engine analyzes my usage and mood. Right now, it sees it's late at night and I've been feeling anxious. It gives me a critical 85% risk of relapse. Instead of letting me fail, it suggests one click to 'Start Focus Mode Now'."

## 1:15 - 2:00 (Mood Journal & AI Companion)
- Navigate to **Mood Journal**.
- Log an entry: "I feel terrible because I wasted 5 hours on TikTok..."
- Hit **Save & Analyze**.
- **Script:** "DigiWell processes my entry locally. It detects 'Catastrophizing' and offers an empathetic reframe. It even gives me a 1-minute breathing micro-task to break the dopamine loop right now."

## 2:00 - 2:45 (Interactive CBT Therapy)
- Navigate to **Therapy**.
- Click **Try Interactive CBT**.
- **Script:** "If I need deeper help, I don't just read a list of tips. I talk to my CBT micro-agent. It uses Socratic questioning to walk me out of my anxiety spike, and then directly generates a curated Commitment for me."

## 2:45 - 3:00 (Focus Enforcement & Conclusion)
- Accept the commitment. See the exact timer start.
- **Script:** "The AI has guided me from a moment of crisis into a protected focus window, dynamically enforcing app blocks. This is the future of digital proactive health. This is DigiWell."
