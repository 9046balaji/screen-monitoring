# Pitch Deck Slides (5)

## 1. Problem
*   We exist in an attention economy designed to monetize our time through endless engagement loops.
*   Consequences: Soaring anxiety, depression, and significant productivity loss globally.
*   Existing tools simply reflect data back at the user without offering meaningful interventional pathways.

## 2. Solution: DigiWell AI
*   A proactive digital wellness ecosystem.
*   Moves beyond passive 'Screen Time' metrics.
*   Integrates predictive modeling to warn users of impending behavioral relapse before it escalates.

## 3. Key Differentiators
*   **Predictive Relapse Defense:** Anticipates doomscrolling spikes based on usage and mood baselines.
*   **CBT Micro-Agent:** Provides immediate Socratic intervention seamlessly inside the block flow.
*   **Dynamic Enforcement:** Seamlessly converts a distressed user's intent directly into a hardlocked Focus session.

## 4. Market Opportunity & Traction
*   Targeting remote workers, knowledge economy professionals, and students.
*   The Mental Wellness & Focus SaaS sector is rapidly expanding.
*   Enterprise tier capabilities: Anonymous telemetry reporting to HR and wellness programs for burnout prevention.

## 5. Vision / Roadmap
*   **V1 (Today):** App blocking + localized CBT AI models + Mood Journal.
*   **V2:** Biometric integrations via wearable APIs for real-time cortisol/stress detection.
*   **V3:** Community accountability and group focus sessions.
