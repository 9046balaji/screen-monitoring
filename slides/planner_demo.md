# Presentation: Weekly Planner to Daily Action Engine

## Slide 1: The Problem
- Planners tell you what to do.
- Focus trackers tell you what you did.
- **Problem**: There's a disconnect. Plans fail upon first contact with reality (or YouTube).

## Slide 2: The DigiWell Solution
- **A Closed-Loop System**: 
  1. Plan the week. 
  2. Generate the daily schedule. 
  3. Let the Agent execute Focus Mode automatically for scheduled deep work.

## Slide 3: Weekly Timetable Engine
- Intuitive drag/drop/modal schedule builder.
- "Focus Mode" toggles built right into the schedule.
- Say goodbye to remembering to launch your website blocker!

## Slide 4: Real-time Execution & Analytics
- 1-Click materialization: Generates "Today's Tasks" from the blueprint.
- Execution loop: Tasks trigger system-wide focus mode constraints. 
- Adherence engine computes ratio = `scheduled_tasks` vs `completed_tasks`.

## Slide 5: Behavioral Coach 
- Evaluates your deviation from your plan.
- Deterministic rules instantly recommend scheduling improvements (e.g., "Shift tasks to morning").
- Optional LLM injects deeper, personalized rationale based on mood and habits.
